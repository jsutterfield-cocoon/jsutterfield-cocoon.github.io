Example 1: Leave-Start boundaries group leave days into complete lower-hour weeks (32h), creating higher percentages (1.0 + 1.0 + 0.2 = 2.2 weeks) compared to Monday-Sunday's mixed approach (0.25 + 1.0 + 0.75 = 2.0 weeks).

Conclusion: With variable schedules, work week boundary choice can impact FMLA usage by 0.15-0.20 weeks, highlighting the importance of consistent boundary definitions for fair administration.
